Payments for the Internet Age

The Hyperledger Value Transfer Protocol (HVTP) is protocol designed from the ground up to enable payments for the internet age. With decentralised nodes running in multiple geographies and jurisdictions, hyperledger provides an amazingly quick, reliable and secure way to record transfers of assets such as digital currencies across the world within seconds, not minutes.
